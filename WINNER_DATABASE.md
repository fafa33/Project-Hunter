# WINNER DATABASE

## Purpose

The Winner Database contains the complete historical record of the greatest crypto investments ever created.

The objective is not documentation.

The objective is understanding why extraordinary winners emerged.

Project Hunter assumes that exceptional future opportunities will share structural characteristics with exceptional historical winners.
