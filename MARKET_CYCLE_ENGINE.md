# MARKET CYCLE ENGINE

## Purpose

The Market Cycle Engine determines where the crypto market currently stands.

Every investment decision must be interpreted within the context of the current market cycle.

The same project can produce completely different outcomes depending on cycle timing.

---

# First Principle

Markets move in cycles.

Projects do not exist independently of the market.

Cycle analysis is mandatory before any investment decision.

---

# Market Cycle Phases

The engine must classify the market into one of the following phases.

Phase 1

Capitulation

Characteristics:

Extreme fear

Low liquidity

Negative sentiment

Heavy selling

Strong value opportunities

---

Phase 2

Accumulation

Characteristics:

Smart money buying

Retail absent

Low volatility

Strong fundamentals ignored

Long-term opportunities appear

---

Phase 3

Expansion

Characteristics:

Capital returns

Narratives emerge

Liquidity improves

Volume increases

Institutional participation grows

---

Phase 4

Acceleration

Characteristics:

Retail participation

Rapid price appreciation

Strong narratives

High confidence

Momentum investing

---

Phase 5

Euphoria

Characteristics:

Extreme optimism

High leverage

Speculation

Unsustainable valuations

Maximum media attention

---

Phase 6

Distribution

Characteristics:

Whale selling

Retail buying

Divergence

Weakening fundamentals

Excessive optimism

---

Phase 7

Bear Market

Characteristics:

Declining liquidity

Narrative collapse

Panic selling

Developer attrition

Weak projects disappear
