# HUNTER ALGORITHM

## Purpose

This document defines the intelligence and decision-making algorithm of Project Hunter.

The objective is NOT to identify good crypto projects.

The objective is to discover statistically significant market mispricing before it becomes obvious.

The system must continuously search for asymmetric opportunities capable of producing extraordinary returns (10x–100x+) while minimizing false positives.

---

# First Principle

Everything starts from probability.

Nothing starts from belief.

Every project begins with a probability of ZERO.

Every probability must be earned through evidence.
