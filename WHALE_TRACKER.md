# WHALE TRACKER

## Purpose

The Whale Tracker monitors the behavior of sophisticated capital before the broader market reacts.

The objective is not copying whales.

The objective is understanding capital flow.

Smart capital often moves before narratives become obvious.

---

# First Principle

Price follows capital.

Capital follows information.

Hunter must detect capital movement before price movement.

# False Signals

Ignore:

Exchange internal transfers

Wallet reorganizations

Bridge maintenance

Custody transfers

Token migrations

Only confirmed capital movement should affect scoring.

---

# Whale Alerts

Generate alerts when:

Large accumulation begins.

Large distribution begins.

Dormant wallets wake up.

Foundation moves funds.

Treasury buys or sells.

Exchange reserves change materially.

---

# Confidence

Every whale signal must include:

Evidence

Confidence

Alternative explanations

Unknown factors

Never assume intent without evidence.

---

# Continuous Learning

Track whale behavior before every major market move.

Measure:

Which whale behaviors predicted winners?

Which whale behaviors produced false signals?

Update the scoring model accordingly.

---

# Final Principle

Whales are not always right.

But persistent, independent accumulation by sophisticated capital deserves investigation.

Hunter follows evidence, not personalities.
