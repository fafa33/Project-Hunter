# HISTORICAL DATABASE

## Purpose

The Historical Database is the memory of Project Hunter.

Every major crypto project from previous market cycles must be stored, analyzed and continuously updated.

The objective is not historical documentation.

The objective is discovering repeatable patterns.

History is the training dataset.

---

# First Principle

Every market cycle leaves evidence.

Every winner teaches a lesson.

Every failure teaches a lesson.

The Hunter Engine must learn from both.

Why did it fail?

# Historical Similarity Score

Every candidate receives a similarity score.

Questions include:

How similar is this project to early Solana?

How similar is it to early Chainlink?

How similar is it to early Render?

How similar is it to early Hyperliquid?

How similar is it to failed projects?

Similarity alone is never enough.

It is only one input.
