# CATALYST TRACKER

## Purpose

The Catalyst Tracker identifies measurable events capable of changing market perception and forcing price repricing.

The objective is not predicting random pumps.

The objective is identifying fundamental events that permanently change valuation.

---

# First Principle

Markets rarely reprice without a catalyst.

Good projects often remain undervalued until a measurable event changes expectations.

Catalysts are the bridge between intrinsic value and market value.
