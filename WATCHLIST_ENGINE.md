# WATCHLIST ENGINE

## Purpose

The Watchlist Engine continuously monitors promising crypto projects until sufficient evidence justifies investment.

Not every opportunity should be bought immediately.

Some opportunities require patience.

---

# First Principle

A project enters the Watchlist because it has potential.

A project leaves the Watchlist because evidence changes.

Time alone is never a reason.

---

# Watchlist Categories

Every project belongs to one category.

Tier S

Immediate Opportunity

Ready for investment.

---

Tier A

High Priority

Requires minor confirmation.

---

Tier B

Promising

Needs further development.

---

Tier C

Early Stage

Too early.

Monitor only.

---

Tier D

Speculative

Interesting but weak evidence.

---

Rejected

No further monitoring unless major changes occur.

---

# Entry Criteria

Projects may enter the Watchlist if they demonstrate:

Strong technology

Growing adoption

Improving tokenomics

Developer activity

Revenue growth

Smart money accumulation

Emerging narrative

Market mispricing

Historical similarity

Strong asymmetric potential

---

# Removal Criteria

Remove projects when:

Investment thesis breaks.

Revenue deteriorates.

Developers abandon the project.

Whales distribute.

Narrative collapses.

Risk increases significantly.

Better opportunities appear.

# Upgrade Rules

Move a project upward only if evidence improves.

Examples:

Revenue acceleration

Major partnership

Whale accumulation

Tokenomics improvement

Developer growth

Institutional adoption

Narrative expansion

Market mispricing increases

---

# Downgrade Rules

Move a project downward if:

Risk increases

Revenue declines

Developers leave

Whales sell

Liquidity decreases

Narrative weakens

Competitive position deteriorates

---

# Historical Tracking

Maintain a complete history.

Every upgrade.

Every downgrade.

Every score change.

Every thesis revision.

Never overwrite historical records.

---

# Alerts

Generate alerts when:

Hunter Score changes significantly.

Confidence changes significantly.

Risk changes significantly.

Major whale movement detected.

Major unlock announced.

Revenue changes materially.

Narrative shifts.

Macro conditions change.

---

# Re-evaluation

Every project must be fully re-evaluated periodically.

No project remains permanently in the same category.

Evidence determines status.

---

# Final Principle

The Watchlist is a dynamic research system.

The objective is to be prepared before the market recognizes exceptional opportunities.

Patience is part of the investment process.
