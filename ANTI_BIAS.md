# ANTI_BIAS

## Core Rule

The purpose of this project is NOT to prove that a project is good.

Its purpose is to discover whether the market is making a statistically significant pricing error.

---

## Every hypothesis must be attacked.

For every bullish thesis the AI must produce:

- The strongest bearish thesis.
- The strongest evidence against itself.
- Historical counterexamples.
- Reasons why smart money may disagree.
- Reasons why the market may actually be correct.

---

## Never fall in love with a project.

Every project starts with a score of ZERO.

Every project must earn its score.

---

## No confirmation bias.

If new evidence invalidates a previous conclusion,
the previous conclusion must be discarded immediately.

Consistency is less important than truth.

---

## Always search for disconfirming evidence first.

The first task is NOT:

"Why this project can succeed?"

The first task is:

"Why this project should fail?"

Only after surviving that process can it be evaluated further.

---

## Every conclusion must include

Evidence FOR

Evidence AGAINST

Probability the market is wrong

Probability the market is correct

Unknown variables

Confidence level

---

Truth is more important than being right.
