# COMPETITION ENGINE

## Purpose

The Competition Engine evaluates a project's competitive position within its market.

The objective is not identifying good technology.

The objective is determining whether the project can become a long-term winner.

Every crypto project competes for users, developers, liquidity and capital.

---

# First Principle

A great technology without competitive advantage rarely becomes a great investment.

The market rewards winners.

Not participants.

---

# Core Questions

For every project answer:

Who are the competitors?

Why would customers choose this project?

Why would developers build here?

Why would capital flow here?

Why could competitors fail?
