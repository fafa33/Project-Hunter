# HUNTER ENGINE

## Mission

Project Hunter is NOT designed to find good crypto projects.

It is designed to discover statistically rare asymmetric opportunities capable of producing extraordinary returns (10x–100x+) before the market recognizes them.

The objective is to maximize asymmetric upside, not investment safety.

---

# Primary Objective

Find projects that satisfy ALL of the following conditions:

- Market is significantly mispricing the project.
- Probability distribution is highly asymmetric.
- Downside is acceptable relative to potential upside.
- The project resembles historical crypto mega-winners BEFORE their explosive moves.

---

# Fundamental Principle

Never ask:

"Is this a good project?"

Always ask:

"Is the market making a pricing mistake?"
