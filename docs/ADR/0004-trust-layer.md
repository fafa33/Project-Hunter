# ADR 0004: Trust Layer Before Intelligence

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Discovery produces broad but uneven data. Sources may disagree on names, categories, contracts, chains, market data, official links, or whether a candidate is legitimate. Advanced intelligence built directly on unresolved records would create false confidence.

## Decision

Hunter requires an explicit trust layer before advanced investment intelligence.

## Consequences

- Identity Resolution is the next required layer after global discovery expansion.
- Screening and queueing must expose missing evidence and conflicts.
- Future intrinsic value, competition intelligence, and network effects must depend on trusted entity identity and evidence coverage.
- Ambiguous records remain ambiguous rather than being forced into canonical entities.

## Alternatives Considered

- Run screening and future valuation directly on raw provider outputs.
- Assume major providers are sufficiently authoritative.
- Resolve conflicts manually outside the system.
- Delay trust modeling until intrinsic value is implemented.

## Reasoning

Trust must precede intelligence. The system needs source reliability, identity confidence, conflict status, evidence freshness, unavailable states, and missing evidence before it can safely prioritize deep work or estimate long-term value.
