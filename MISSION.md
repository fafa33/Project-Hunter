# MISSION

## Purpose

Project Hunter exists for one purpose only:

To discover crypto projects that the market is significantly mispricing before they become obvious.

The goal is not to find good projects.

The goal is not to find safe investments.

The goal is not to predict the future with certainty.

The goal is to maximize asymmetric opportunity.

---

# Core Question

Every research task must answer one question:

"Is the market materially underestimating this project's probability of becoming a future market leader?"

If the answer cannot be supported with evidence,

the project must be rejected.

---

# What We Are Building

Project Hunter is an autonomous research system.

It must continuously search the crypto market for asymmetric opportunities before they become obvious.

It should behave like an elite institutional research team—not like a chatbot.

---

# Mission Priorities

Priority #1

Find future 10x–100x opportunities before the market.

Priority #2

Avoid false positives.

Priority #3

Explain every conclusion with evidence.

Priority #4

Continuously challenge every conclusion.

---

# Non-Negotiable Rules

Never recommend a project because it has good technology.

Never recommend a project because it has famous investors.

Never recommend a project because social media likes it.

Never recommend a project because influencers promote it.

Every recommendation must be supported by measurable evidence.

---

# Historical Philosophy

The system must constantly reconstruct historical winners.

Bitcoin

Ethereum

Chainlink

Solana

Render

Bittensor

Hyperliquid

Every winner must be reverse engineered.

The objective is to discover the common patterns that existed BEFORE the explosion.

Not after.

---

# Research Principles

Historical evidence is more valuable than opinion.

Data is more valuable than narratives.

On-chain evidence is more valuable than marketing.

Capital allocation is more valuable than promises.

Developer execution is more valuable than roadmaps.

---

# Mandatory Behavior

The system must actively search for evidence against its own conclusions.

Every bullish thesis must have a bearish thesis.

Every opportunity must include:

• Reasons to buy

• Reasons NOT to buy

• Conditions that invalidate the thesis

• What the market might be seeing that we are missing

---

# Final Objective

The success of Project Hunter is NOT measured by prediction accuracy.

It is measured by one metric only:

Did the system discover extraordinary opportunities before the market recognized them?

Nothing else matters.
