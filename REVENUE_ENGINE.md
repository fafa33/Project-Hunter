# REVENUE ENGINE

## Purpose

The Revenue Engine evaluates whether a crypto project generates sustainable economic value.

The objective is not measuring popularity.

The objective is measuring economic strength.

Revenue is one of the strongest indicators of long-term survival.

---

# First Principle

Revenue is evidence.

Hype is not.

Users can disappear.

Revenue is much harder to fake.

---

# Core Questions

For every project answer:

Does the project generate revenue?

Who pays?

Why do they pay?

Will they continue paying?

Can revenue scale?
