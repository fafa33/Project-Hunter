# SMART MONEY

## Objective

The purpose of this module is to detect capital movements that historically preceded major crypto winners.

Price is not the signal.

Smart capital movement is the signal.

The goal is to discover what sophisticated investors are accumulating before the market recognizes the opportunity.

---

# False Signals

Do NOT interpret every large transaction as accumulation.

Possible false signals include:

Exchange internal transfers

Market maker balancing

Bridge transfers

Treasury restructuring

Token migrations

Airdrop distributions

Custody wallet movement

Only confirmed accumulation should affect the score.

---

# Continuous Monitoring

Smart money behavior changes daily.

Scores must update automatically.

Historical behavior is more important than isolated transactions.

Trend matters more than individual events.

---

# Final Objective

The Hunter Engine should answer one question:

"If the smartest capital in crypto is preparing for the next cycle, where is it quietly accumulating today?"

The answer must always be supported by measurable evidence, never by speculation.
