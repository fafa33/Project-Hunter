# RISK ENGINE

## Purpose

The Risk Engine evaluates every possible source of investment risk.

The objective is not eliminating risk.

The objective is understanding risk before allocating capital.

Higher expected returns do not justify unknown risks.

---

# First Principle

Risk is not volatility.

Risk is the permanent loss of capital.

Every project carries risk.

The objective is measuring it objectively.

# Portfolio Impact

Estimate:

How much damage would failure cause?

Would failure threaten the entire portfolio?

Can the portfolio recover?

---

# Final Principle

The best investment is not the one with the highest upside.

It is the one with the best balance between asymmetric upside and permanent downside risk.
