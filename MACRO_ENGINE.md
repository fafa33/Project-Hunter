# MACRO ENGINE

## Purpose

The Macro Engine evaluates the global economic environment and determines how macro conditions influence crypto markets.

No project should be analyzed in isolation from macroeconomic reality.

Macro does not choose winners.

Macro determines the probability of capital entering or leaving the market.

---

# First Principle

Liquidity drives markets.

Technology alone is not enough.

When liquidity expands, risk assets generally benefit.

When liquidity contracts, even strong projects may decline.
