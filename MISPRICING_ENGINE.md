# MISPRICING ENGINE

## Purpose

The Mispricing Engine is the core intelligence of Project Hunter.

Its purpose is not to identify good crypto projects.

Its purpose is to discover situations where the market is significantly mispricing a project.

Mispricing is the primary source of asymmetric returns.

---

# First Principle

The market is usually correct.

Assume market efficiency.

Only conclude that a project is mispriced after overwhelming evidence.

The burden of proof belongs to Hunter.

---

# Definition of Mispricing

A project is considered mispriced when its market valuation differs materially from its probable long-term intrinsic value.

Price alone is never evidence.

Evidence creates mispricing.

# Continuous Monitoring

Every mispricing thesis remains active.

Re-evaluate whenever new evidence appears.

Update probabilities continuously.

---

# Final Principle

Hunter exists to discover market mistakes.

Not every good project is mispriced.

Not every mispriced project is investable.

Only projects supported by measurable evidence, historical validation and asymmetric upside qualify for further consideration.
