# NARRATIVE ENGINE

## Purpose

The Narrative Engine identifies emerging market narratives before they become mainstream.

The objective is not to follow hype.

The objective is to detect narratives while they are still underpriced.

Project Hunter assumes that major crypto cycles are driven by narrative rotation.

---

# First Principle

Technology creates products.

Narratives create demand.

Capital follows narratives.

The best investments often occur before the narrative becomes popular.

---

# Narrative Lifecycle

Every narrative progresses through stages.

Stage 1

Research

Very few people are aware.

Mostly developers and researchers.

---

Stage 2

Early Adoption

Builders enter.

Smart money begins accumulating.

Retail remains absent.

---

Stage 3

Recognition

Industry begins discussing the topic.

Media attention increases.

Capital starts flowing.

---

Stage 4

Expansion

Narrative becomes dominant.

Retail enters.

Large price appreciation occurs.

---

Stage 5

Euphoria

Every project claims to belong.

Valuations disconnect from reality.

Capital floods the sector.

---

Stage 6

Exhaustion

Growth slows.

Attention declines.

Capital rotates elsewhere.

# Narrative Rotation

Track movement of attention between narratives.

Questions include:

Which narratives are losing momentum?

Which narratives are accelerating?

Which narratives remain undiscovered?

Where is capital moving next?

---

# False Narratives

Reject narratives supported only by:

Influencer promotion

Celebrity endorsement

Short-term price appreciation

Unverified partnerships

Marketing campaigns

Narratives without measurable adoption.

# Project Alignment

For every project determine:

Does it genuinely belong to the narrative?

---

# Final Principle

Do not chase narratives.

Discover them before the market does.

The goal is to identify the next dominant crypto narrative while it is still being ignored.
